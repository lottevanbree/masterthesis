Master thesis UX Design

Survey on how vocabulary influences the user experience

I am currently conducting a research study as part of my master thesis on UX design. The focus of my study is to investigate the influence of vocabulary on user experience. Your participation in this survey will provide valuable insights and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in the field of UX design.Please be assured that all data collected will be handled with the utmost confidentiality and anonymity. The information you provide will only be used for the purpose of this research study and will not be shared with any third party. Participation in this survey is entirely voluntary, and you are free to withdraw at any time without any penalty.

Your valuable input is greatly appreciated, and your time and effort in completing the survey are highly valued. Thank you for your willingness to participate in this study.
