Master thesis UX Design

Survey of the user experience within music streaming services

For this study we focus on the user experience within music streaming services focussing on playlists. This survey will take about 10 minutes to complete.

Requirements

  • You need to be active on any sort of music streaming service (e.g. Spotify, last.fm, AppleMusic).

Privacy policy

Your participation in this survey will provide valuable insights and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in the field of UX design. All data collected will be handled with confidentiality and anonymity. The information you provide will only be used for the purpose of this research study and will not be shared with any third party.
To ensure that the research data we collect is accurate and reliablle, we ask you to be completley honest in your response. Participation in this survey is entirely voluntary, and you are free to withdraw at any time.
If you have read the above-mentioned information and want to take part in this study, consent by clicking on the "Continue" button.
